### VMware Workstation

- You can download VMware Workstation from the here:

### Red Hat 7.9 ISO

- You can download the ISO from Red Hat Offical Website here.

### Host System Requirements

- Minimum 8 GB of RAM.
- A Dual Core Processor.
- 128 GB+ of Hard Drive Storage
